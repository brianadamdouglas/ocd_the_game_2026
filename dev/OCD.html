You are running late for work. You have 3 minutes to get out the front door to make it on time. Are you sure you have not left a dangerous situation behind?



How to play


Press and hold the UP arrow to walk forward.
Press the LEFT arrow to turn left.
Press the RIGHT arrow to turn right.
Press the DOWN arrow to turn around.

Press the A button to interact with objects. This includes both stationary and movable objects. Not everything in the space is interactive.

Press the B button to pick up objects. Not everything in the space can be lifted.
